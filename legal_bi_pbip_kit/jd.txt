Why Open
The team is driving a firm-wide BI transformation, consolidating reporting from Cognos, SSRS, and other legacy systems into a modern Power BI + Databricks ecosystem. Hence, needing a Power BI Specialist.
This is a high-impact, highly visible role with the opportunity to shape how analytics is delivered across the business while working closely with stakeholders on critical reporting needs.

Overview
We are virtually looking for a Power BI Specialist and this role reports directly to Ilya Eydis and sits within a core BI team responsible for delivering business-facing analytics products, primarily dashboards and light data models on the Data Engineering team. The focus is on the front-end BI layer, this is not a data engineering role.
The hire will work in the end-user analytics and reporting space, partnering with product managers and business stakeholders to gather requirements, shape reporting needs, and translate those into deliverables that engineering teams can support.

What the Role Focuses On
Designing and building Power BI dashboards and reports
Developing semantic/data models where needed
Translating business requirements into technical specifications
Partnering with stakeholders (Finance, Marketing, etc.) to define KPIs
Collaborating with data engineers when new data ingestion is required
Supporting migration from legacy tools (Cognos, SSRS)
Breakdown: ~80% hands-on development, ~20% stakeholder engagement

Success in the First 6 Months
Deliver high-quality, production-ready dashboards

Contribute to migration of legacy reporting into Power BI

Strong hire: operates independently, produces polished outputs quickly, engages stakeholders well
